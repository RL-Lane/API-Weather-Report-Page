Humidity Map

This map shows a heatmap of humidity for all cities within the dataset.

Two cities near each other can contribute just as much to the heatmap as having higher humidity with fewer cities. One way to defeat this problem would be to evenly space points, but weather data may be more commonly recorded in cities large enough to have weather stations.

Top Cities for Good Weather

Here, top cities are selected which meet the following criteria:

  • Max temp between 70°F and 80°F
  • Wind speed under 10 mph
  • Cloudless skies

Keep in mind that this is only pertaining to the date and time that the query was run for the project python-api-challenge: 30 April, 2022, 2:38 AM CDT.

Top Pick

Of the 14 cities which matched the previous criteria, 3 of them landed in the tiny island nation of Mauritius.

This was an island I had not heard of. It seems to have a good blend of weather along with citizens who have a fairly good quality of life and fair income. This nation will most certainly be on my mind if I can afford to travel.
